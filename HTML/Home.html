Macapagal Sari-sari Store

Established in 2002, the brief history of the firm is that after the owner's wife died, he decided to start a business out of boredom and loneliness. He anticipated that establishing a sari-sari store in front of a basketball court would be a good investment because many children would be loitering.

Several years, months, days, seconds have passed and the store was a really huge success. Business was booming left and right. That's how the story started for this simple sari-sari store.
